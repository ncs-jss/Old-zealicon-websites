Ever seen mega machines lift tonnes of load like grams??? Yes that’s the power of water and its science is hydraulics.The reason is simple – Water is incompressible and the law behind transmission of such huge amount of force is the very basic – PASCAL’S LAW. Keeping the basics at core, we present to you Hydronoid Level 2.0, an opportunity to make a mobile hydraulic crane. Energize your machine with syringes filled with water and discover the joy of playing with the basics of our apparently complicated science. Get ready for the battle as Hydronoid gets bigger, better and tougher
