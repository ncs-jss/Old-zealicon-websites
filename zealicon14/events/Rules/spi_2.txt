RULES-  

1. Each team can consist of a maximum of 4 members. 
2. Each team should have unique participants i.e. no two teams can have even a single participant common. 
3. The team members can be from different institutes or colleges. 
4. All participants should carry a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes during the event. 
5. The right spirit of participation is expected from the participants. 
6. The decision of the JUDGES shall be final and binding.
