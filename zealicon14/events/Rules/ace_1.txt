•	Rule No. 1-Teams will be given 2 minutes to make a closed structure by cardboard bricks.
•	Rule No. 2- Opposite team will get 2 throws to destroy the structure from a distance of 12m.
•	Rule No. 3- 1 point will be awarded for the bricks falling inside the structure and 2 points will be awarded for the bricks falling outside the structure.
•	Rule No. 4- Team with maximum score wins.
