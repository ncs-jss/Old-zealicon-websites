•	Each group will comprise of 10 participants.
•	The group will be given 2 minutes to prepare for the topic
        given on the spot.
•	10 minutes will be given to the group to discuss the given 
        topic.
•	After 10 minutes, each participant will be asked to 
        summarize the discussion in a minute.
•	Selection for the semi-final and final rounds will be done on 
        an individual level.
