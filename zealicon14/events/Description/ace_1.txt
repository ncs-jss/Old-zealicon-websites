Cardboard bricks of dimensions 20cmX10cmX10cm will be provided to make a closed structure 60cm high on all sides. All the members of a team will be allowed to make the structure in 2 minutes time. 2 members of the opposite team will throw balls at the structure to get maximum number of bricks to fall on ground. 

•	Point No. 1- Allotted area for making the structure is 2mX1.5m.
•	Point No. 2- Throw will be taken from a distance of 12m.
