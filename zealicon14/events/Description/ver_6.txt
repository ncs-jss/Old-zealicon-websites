Drama is the only way to read ‘the book of people’, not merely as confounded philosophy but as the exhibition of human character. Dramatics is not just about proficient writing or acting, but also about the ability to articulate human relationships and enunciate the plethora of emotions that are associated with basic social existence. Encompassing the myriad hues of life , PRATIBIMBA – The stage play is a definite destination for all the theatre lovers.
